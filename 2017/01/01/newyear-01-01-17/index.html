New Year

2017/01/01
  • GoMagpye
  • nes classic

A year from now, I am going to look back at this, my first blog, and say “What the hell was I doing?”. What was I thinking. I haven’t had a clue in the world on how blogging works. But I think I’m going to like it. I hear its very therapeutic, right? You write about what you love and have passion about. Lets see how it turns out.

Things are moving faster. Things are coming to GoMagpye. A “yuuuuuuuuuge” update will soon be rolling out. We got some feedback from our Beta users. Thank you guys. And we listened and have made changes all around. The site feels almost like a new site. Ebay and Target are coming soon as well. I can’t wait for that. Too go broke lol. I was alerted so many times during the holidays about a price of an item going down by our site GoMagpye I think I might have gone broke. If it wasnt for our site, it would be very difficult to keep track of prices going down on certain items and miss out on a deal. You would not believe how many times during the holidays these stores do flash sales at different days and at different hours. I would not have been able to tell you this next success story if I was not alerted on time.

The NES Classic. This bad boy was difficult to get a hold off but I got it through Walmart one mid-afternoon during work. I couldn’t believe it. My phone just buzzed with an sms text message and email alerting me within a minute that the NES Classic was available. Thank god because within minutes the console sold out(again). I am glad I got it and I hope to hear more success stories like these from people who got alerted about a deal or notified when the hot item went on sale.
