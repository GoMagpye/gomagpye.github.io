Hello World

2016/12/29

Welcome to GoMagpye! This is our very first blog post, of many, hopefully. Our mission is to provide the best online shopping experience for you. We do this by aggregating a growing number of online stores and giving our awesome users the ability to get notified when an item’s price changes or when an item becomes available. We try to ensure that you never miss a deal or an item that you’re looking for.

The purpose of our blog is to keep you up-to-date with our most trending products. We’ll tell you everything you need to know for you to make an informed decision before you buy. And since we’re a bootstrapped startup made up of a bunch of wizards, we’re also going to be writing about our experience growing the company. We’ll discuss technology stacks and why we made certain decisions, and why we regret those decisions. We’re also constantly making updates based on your feedback, so be sure to check out this blog regularly for information on awesome new features.

Our promise to you is to deliver the best ecosystem for online shoppers and share with you everything we’ve learned along the way. We hope you’ll join and engage with us on this adventure.
